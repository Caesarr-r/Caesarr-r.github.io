About

I am an undergraduate student at Shandong University (2023–2027), working on graph machine learning. My past and current research mainly focuses on text-attributed graphs (TAG).

Within TAG, I have explored several reliability-related topics, where out-of-distribution (OOD) detection is one small direction. This line of work led to my recent paper accepted by Knowledge-Based Systems (KBS).

My future research will focus more on multimodal attributed graphs (MAG).

Research

  • Main track (Past & Current): Text-Attributed Graphs (TAG)
  • Sub-direction: OOD detection for TAG
  • Future direction: Multimodal Attributed Graphs (MAG)
