About

I am currently pursuing my B.Eng. degree at Shandong University (2023–2027). I work on machine learning for graph-structured data, with a particular interest in out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization and data-centric graph intelligence.

My goal is to design practical and theoretically grounded methods that remain robust when deployment conditions differ from training distributions, especially for text-attributed graphs.

Research Focus

Graph Machine Learning

Representation learning and robust reasoning on attributed and text-enhanced graphs.

OOD Detection

Few-shot and distribution-shift settings for reliable deployment in open environments.

Data-Centric AI

Improving model reliability through data quality, topology-text consistency, and evaluation protocols.
