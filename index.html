About

I am an undergraduate student at Shandong University (2023–2027), working on graph learning. My current research mainly focuses on text-attributed graphs (TAG).

Within this main line, out-of-distribution (OOD) detection is a relatively small sub-direction, which led to our recent KBS-accepted paper.

My future research will focus more on multimodal attributed graphs (MAG).

Research

  • Main track (Current): Text-Attributed Graphs (TAG)
  • Sub-direction: OOD detection for TAG
  • Future direction: Multimodal Attributed Graphs (MAG)
